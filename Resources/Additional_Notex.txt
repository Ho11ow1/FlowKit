■ FlowKit Prefab - Additional Documentation
================================

■ Recommended usage
--------------
○ General
  ・ PersistentCanvas
    ・ Simple persistent canvas which will never get destroyed
  ・ Timer
    ・ Time-restricted gameplay segments | Puzzles / Survival segments
  ・ OnlineClock
    ・ Specific time events | world bosses / world resets

○ Sandbox
  ○ DatingSim
    ・ SandboxDatingInterface
      ・ Interface to display character data (Character data can be inherited and expanded)
    ・ SandboxDatingToggle
      ・ Provides a simple toggle button for displaying the SandboxDatingInterface or your own
    ・ SandboxDatingInterfaceSystem
      ・ Combines the Interface and Toggle for ease of use
  ○ Time
    ・ SandboxClock
      ・ Time management with UI display
    ・ SandboxClockNV
      ・ Works exactly the same as SandboxClock but without a visual representation
    ・ SandboxDateCycle
      ・ Day / Week time managament (Best combined with a SandboxClock for time managed events)
